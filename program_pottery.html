Introduction to Pottery

Cassy Lang


In this hands-on workshop you will learn the basic tools and materials used to make hand-built ceramics.
Students will learn these fundamental skills:

  • Pinching
  • Coiling with banding wheel
  • Slab preparation with the slab roller
  • Wedging

By the end of this workshop, students will have created at least three ceramics projects:

  • pinch pot
  • small slab plate
  • coil bowl
